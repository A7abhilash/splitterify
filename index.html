More Features

Splitterify is a neatly build bill splitter app to split the bill among friends going on vacation/outing and stop fighting over who needs to pay who.

  • Minimalistic UI
  • Sign up for Free
  • Update profile and Manage your account
  • Keep your Virtual Payment Address(UPI ID) upto date to receive payments
  • Helpful to save money
